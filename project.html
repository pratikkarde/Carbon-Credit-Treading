Amazon Rainforest Conservation

Forest Conservation VCS Certified
India

Project Overview

This project aims to protect 50,000 hectares of pristine Amazon rainforest from deforestation. Through a combination of conservation efforts, community engagement, and sustainable development initiatives, the project will prevent the release of approximately 250,000 tonnes of CO2e over its 30-year crediting period.

Environmental Impact

Key Environmental Benefits

  • Protection of biodiversity and wildlife habitats
  • Preservation of indigenous plant species
  • Maintenance of water cycles and soil quality
  • Reduction of forest fragmentation

Social Impact

Community Benefits

  • Creation of sustainable employment opportunities
  • Support for local education initiatives
  • Development of eco-tourism programs
  • Training in sustainable forest management

Project Location

The project is located in the heart of the Amazon rainforest, specifically in the state of Amazonas, Brazil. The protected area spans multiple municipalities and is home to diverse ecosystems and indigenous communities.
